Departments & Courses

DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The Department of Information Technology of SIBA Campus offers a number of well designed study programs in English Medium for school leavers seeking higher education and for those who seek career development through IT proficiency.The courses are conducted by a dedicated, well-qualified panel of full-time and visiting lecturers. The Department has well equipped computer laboratories for students to get hands-on experience, and modern teaching tools to support teaching.

COURSES
  • BSC in Information Technology
  • BTECH in Information Technology & Communication
  • BSC IT (TOP-UP) HND Holders
  • Higher Deploma In IT
  • Deploma In IT

DEPARTMENT OF MANAGEMENT STUDIES

Department of Management studies is set up to promote high quality programs in the field of Business Management. The Diploma, Advanced Diploma, Higher Diploma, and Undergraduate programs offered are designed to develop and enhance knowledge in Management and other related disciplines with a variety of capabilities and soft skills along with critical thinking and analysis, research, cooperation, and communication. Our ultimate goal lies in creating individuals with the right set of knowledge, skills and qualities.

COURSES
  • BBM Degree
  • BA. General Degree
  • Higher Deploma In Business Management
  • Deploma In Business Management

DEPARTMENT OF ENGLISH AND MODERN LANGUAGES

The Department of English and Modern Languages has been one of the strongest departments of SIBA since its beginning, which boasts the highest number of diplomates of SIBA. The department provides Diplomas in English, French, Chinese, Hindi, and Tamil. Apart from the diplomas, the Department of English offers English as a subject for all the Undergraduate Programs SIBA proffers. All the academics in the department are well qualified and experienced in their relevant language fields and are highly competent at second and foreign language teaching which results in catering to the educational needs of all the students who follow the courses the department offers.

COURSES
  • B.TECH Degree
  • Higher Deploma In English
  • Deploma In English

DEPARTMENT OF COUNSELLING PSYCHOLOGY

The Department of Counselling Psychology of Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy is always willing to prepare highly knowledgeable, skilled, dedicated, and ethical professional practitioners, who will endeavor to enhance human potential throughout the life span and who can effectively practice within a variety of settings. The Department of Counselling Psychology promotes counselling as an effective, viable means of assisting individuals throughout the life span. A counselor, regardless of his or her theoretical stance or work setting, functions as a change agent who is sensitive to and knowledgeable about the range of human development reflected in individual differences and cultural, political and linguistic diversity.

COURSES
  • Higher Deploma In Bhuddhist Counselling
  • BA. General Degree in Counselling Psychology
