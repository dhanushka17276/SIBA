Welcome To Education

Sri Lanka International Buddhist Academy (SIBA Campus) is the unique liberal arts Higher Education Institute of Sri Dalada Maligawa, which was set up within a monastic and eco-friendly environment. This is an English Medium co-educational institute that currently facilitated higher education for more than 2000 local and foreign students. It is both national and international in scope, grounded in Buddhist principles and, designed to produce local, national, and international religious, social, and political leaders who are at the same time Dhamma practitioners and disseminates.
